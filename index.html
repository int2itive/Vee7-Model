Membership Tiers:

Seed (Free/Low-Cost): Entry-level benefits including group development webinars, directory listings, basic support, and access to an international directory of artists and ethical businesses.

Growth (£15-25/mo): Includes one-on-one development sessions, booking opportunities, enhanced support, event discounts, and expanded access to the international directory for networking and collaboration.

Elevate (£40-60/mo): Monthly coaching, priority booking, press/media pack creation, advanced networking, funding assistance, plus premium access and featured listings in the international directory.

Legacy (£100+/mo): Premium services including business audits, bespoke project support, VIP networking, exclusive investment opportunities, and top-tier priority placement in the international directory.
